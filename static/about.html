About YSC

Knowledge from Highschoolers to the Highschoolers

YSC provides various seminars and training to Highschoolers in the Nepal. Upgrade your skills through us from novice to expert on various sub-domains of artificial intelligence and robotics.

YSC's mission is to create a platform for the youngsters to take in, showcase, upskill the grip on the highly flourishing world of Arificial Intelligence and Robotics.

It all start with why not we youth are always enthusiastic and eager to learn new and contribute in the society.

YSC is a bridge between these youth and innovation to create a skilled society which we all want.
